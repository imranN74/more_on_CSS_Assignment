Google Font


Google Fonts (formerly known as Google Web Fonts) is a computer font and web font service owned by Google. This includes free and open source font families, an interactive web directory for browsing the library, and APIs for using the fonts via CSS and Android.

Google Fonts is a free and extensive library of web fonts provided by Google. Launched in 2010, it offers a diverse collection of typefaces for use in web design and development. With over a thousand font families, Google Fonts caters to various styles and languages, enhancing the aesthetic appeal and readability of websites. The fonts are easily accessible through a simple integration process, allowing developers to enhance the visual appeal of their websites without compromising on performance. Google Fonts has become a popular choice among designers and developers globally, contributing to the accessibility and creative possibilities of online content.
